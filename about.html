About the Englewood Parent Resource Guide

The information included here was compiled through volunteer efforts on behalf of the Hyde Park/Kenwood CAC. We are grateful for the time and insight of all those who contributed to its production, especially the University of Chicago student organization, Block 58. Thank you to MAPSCorps for their extensive data collection on the South Side. Every effort was made to collect as comprehensive information as possible and to the best of our knowledge, information was current as of April 28, 2015. We apologize for any errors or omissions and will look forward to including updated information in future editions.

This map is based on Derek Eder's Searchable Map Template using Google Fusion Tables.
